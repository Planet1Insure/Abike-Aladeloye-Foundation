Support To Do More

Your support is the bridge that connects ambitious, less-privileged individuals to the **formal and informal education** they need for a sustainable future.

Where Your Contribution Goes

Every donation fuels a direct, measurable impact on a young life. We focus on two key areas where funding is desperately needed:

Formal Education (Scholarships)

Funding covers tuition fees, school uniforms, books, and essential educational supplies for students from primary through tertiary levels. Your support keeps a child in the classroom.

Informal Education (Skills Acquisition)

Donations provide the necessary tools, materials, and apprenticeship stipends for our vocational training programs (like tailoring and fashion design), securing immediate job readiness.
